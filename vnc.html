Step 2 - Accessing VNC's


To connect to VNC's is the easy part, first you will need to install a VNC client, I recommended TigerVNC.

Once you have done that and found a target VNC to connect to from either the two websites or the IoT search engine's, all you have to do is copy the IP and port of the VNC.


(Image from vnc.wtf)


Then finally paste the IP and port into the TigerVNC client and simply click connect.
